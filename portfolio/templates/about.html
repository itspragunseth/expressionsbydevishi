I Am Devishi Seth

I, Devishi Seth am a Painting and Sculpture student at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago (SAIC) in Chicago, Illinois. I continue to explore the possibilities that lie in the world of art in the form of experimenting with different mediums or concepts in the world. I have always observed the world, this society from an outer perspective, questioning what part I could possible play in it. Then I realised that through art, the reflection of my feelings and experiences, I am able to put my thoughts into action and make my mark on the world.
